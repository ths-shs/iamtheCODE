Facilitator Orientation

The iamtheCODE Facilitators Orientation held on February 07, 2025, at the JHS Computer Laboratory served as a meaningful gathering for teachers dedicated to guiding learners through the program. During the session, facilitators aligned on objectives, strategies, and session plans to ensure a smooth and impactful implementation of all modules. The meeting strengthened collaboration, clarified roles, and reinforced the shared commitment to providing students with an inspiring and supportive digital learning experience.
